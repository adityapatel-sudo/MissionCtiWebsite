Who We Are

Mission CTI offers courses in Scratch, Python, C, and Java!

Launched in April of 2020, co-presidents Sanjhee Gupta and Charlotte Chau hope to improve the diversity in computer science and provide opportunities for many students to learn how to code!

Mission CTI is committed to free classes and refined curriculum for all its language courses.















What will my child’s class experience be like?

Mission CTI offers courses in Scratch, Python, C, and Java!

Students will be given projects and practice to grow their skills!

Depending on the course of your choice, instructors will host help sessions every week to provide help for students who need it.
