About Us

About Us

• Our organization has taught over 100 students since our founding in 2020.
• Our goal is to create a world that is fully technologically literate.
• We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

Mission CTI was created at the start of the Covid-19 Pandemic, as a way to help students, help students discover programming and help students to approach common problems from the perspective of a programmer. The teachers of Mission CTI classes all have several years of experience in their dedicated languages and follow a strict course plan to help maximize learning in the classroom. With most of the teachers being students themselves we fully understand how to maximize communication through a computer screen. If you have any thought, questions, or concerns please email us at ctimsjhs@gmail.com
